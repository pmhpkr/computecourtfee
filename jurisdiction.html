Reference — Case Categories (Office Order No. 3/GSTAT/PB/2026)
Category I — Tax Demand & Substantive Issues
  • Misclassification of goods or services
  • Wrong applicability of a notification
  • Incorrect determination of time / value of supply
  • Incorrect admissibility or denial of ITC / blocking of credit
  • Liability to pay tax; registration requirement
  • Determination of tax not paid or short paid — Section 73 or 74
  • Excess ITC availed / utilised — Section 73 or 74
Category II — Registration, Refund & Assessment
  • Rejection / acceptance of registration applications or amendments
  • Suspension, cancellation or revocation of registration
  • Composition scheme denial; GSTP disqualification
  • Recovery, protective assessment, non-filer assessment
  • Refund orders (provisional, rejected, withheld, granted)
  • Provisional assessment; re-credit in credit ledger
Category III — Seizure, Penalty & Residual
  • Seizure / confiscation of goods, books or property
  • Rectification / withdrawal of an earlier order
  • Provisional attachment of property
  • Penalty orders; compounding of offences
  • Any other matter not covered under Category I or II
Note: All cases initially listed before a Division Bench; referred to Single Bench only if the Division Bench finds no question of law is involved (amount < Rs 50 lakhs).
Disclaimer: Jurisdiction is based on S.O. 5063(E) dated 26 November 2024 (as amended) and Office Order No. 3/GSTAT/PB/2026 dated 14 May 2026. Bench composition and sitting arrangements are subject to change. Verify current arrangements from the official GSTAT portal or registry before filing.
