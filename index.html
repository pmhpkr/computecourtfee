Disclaimer: Pre-deposit under Section 107(6)(b) and Section 112(8)(b) is computed at 10% of the disputed tax or ITC amount, subject to a maximum of Rs 20 crore per Act per stage. The cap applies separately to each Act at each stage. For Section 112, the "pre-deposit already paid" figure shown is what you enter — this calculator does not verify or adjust for any partial relief granted at the first appeal stage; you should enter only the amount actually paid and retained (i.e. not already refunded or adjusted). Penalty or interest does not form part of the pre-deposit calculation for a tax demand. For penalty-only orders: no pre-deposit was required for appeals filed before 1 October 2025; a 10% pre-deposit on the penalty amount is required for appeals filed on or after 1 October 2025 (Finance Act, 2025). For interest / fee only orders: no pre-deposit is required. Do not rely solely on this calculation. Always verify against the applicable provisions and the relevant order before making payment.
