SCN & Order Time Limits

Last dates to issue a Show Cause Notice and pass an Order under Section 73 (non-fraud) and Section 74 (fraud / wilful misstatement) for FY 2017-18 to 2023-24.

Rs
Disclaimer: The dates shown are based on Section 73 and 74 of the CGST Act, 2017 as amended, and applicable notifications issued under Section 168A. Dates for FY 2017-18 to 2019-20 reflect one-time extensions granted for COVID-19. Dates marked SCN refer to the last date to issue a show cause notice; Order dates refer to the last date to pass an adjudication order. Section 73 SCN must precede the order date by at least 3 months; Section 74 SCN by at least 6 months. These dates do not account for department-specific circulars, state-level notifications, or orders passed in pending proceedings. Verify against current notifications before relying on any date.
