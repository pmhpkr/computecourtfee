Disclaimer: Section 107(1) provides a three-month limitation period for first appeals from the date of receipt of the order. The first appellate authority may condone delay up to a further one month on sufficient cause (S. 107(4)). Section 112(1) provides a three-month limitation for appeals to GSTAT, with condonation of up to a further three months (S. 112(4)). Limitation periods are subject to exclusion for time spent in legal proceedings (S. 14, Limitation Act). Court holidays and gazette notifications may further affect the deadline. This tool is indicative only — verify the applicable limitation against the statute and case law before filing.
