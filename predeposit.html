Disclaimer: Pre-deposit under Section 107(6)(b) is computed at 10% of the disputed tax or ITC amount (max Rs 20 crore per Act). Under Section 112(8)(b) for GSTAT appeals, it is 20% of the remaining disputed amount (max Rs 50 crore per Act), in addition to what was paid at the Section 107 stage. The cap applies separately to each Act at each stage. For penalty-only orders: no pre-deposit was required for appeals filed before 1 October 2025; a 10% pre-deposit on the penalty amount is required for appeals filed on or after 1 October 2025 (Finance Act, 2025). For interest / fee only orders: no pre-deposit is required. Do not rely solely on this calculation.
